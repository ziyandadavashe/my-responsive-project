why Mafikizolo

Why is Mafikizolo the best duo?

Mafikizolo is an award-winning South African music duo. With a career spanning more than 20 years, selling in excess of 1 million units in that time, the group turned humble beginnings and a love for song into one of the most formative sounds on the African content. Widely regarded as one of the greatest groups in South African music history, members Nhlanhla Nene and Theo Kgosinkwe have remained a mainstay in a fast moving industry due to an ever-evolving sound that continues to capture a new young audience, while retaining the fans who’ve loved them since day one.

What sets Mafikizolo apart is their unique blend of Kwaito music with a touch of Afro-pop, their stylish fashion sense, and their ability to evolve while staying true to their sound. They have achieved significant commercial success, won numerous awards, and maintained a strong presence in the South African music scene for over two decades.

The duo has received nominations for prestigious awards like the BET Awards and MTV Europe Music Awards, highlighting their international appeal.
